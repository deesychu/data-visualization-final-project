# Get Out! A Data-Driven Narrative of Housing Displacement in San Francisco
By: Adrienne Dominique Sy Chu

San Francisco has long struggled with a housing crisis that has forced thousands of residents out of their homes. Eviction notices tell the story of who is being displaced, where it is happening, and why, but raw data alone can be hard to understand. This project, Get Out!, turns that data into a visual story that anyone can follow. Using eviction records from the San Francisco Rent Board, it walks users through three decades of displacement patterns across the city's neighborhoods. The goal is simple: to make the numbers feel real and help everyday people understand one of San Francisco's most pressing issues.
